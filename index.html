Profile

Dr. Wang Ning has been actively involved in research and development of advanced materials and devices for hydrogen energy and fuel cells in recent years. His work focuses on integrating key materials such as electrolytes and air electrodes into proton ceramic cells (proton ceramic electrolysis cells and fuel cells). Furthermore, he is dedicated to leveraging artificial intelligence and large databases to construct machine learning models that accelerate the iterative creation of critical materials for proton ceramic cells.
